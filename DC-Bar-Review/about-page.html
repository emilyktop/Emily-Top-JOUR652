What are we about?

Welcome to the home of D.C. Sports Bar Reviews. Ever wonder which sports bars are the best for you? This is the place to find out. Are you looking for a team-affiliated bar? Are you looking for a bar that has all the games on? Do you want to know if the bar is willing to play the game you want to watch? Will you be able to grab a drink at the bar or will you have to wait forever to get an overpriced brewski? Here we will review as many D.C. bars as possible so that you can find the right bar for your sports viewing occasion.


Who are we?

Our writers here are no one special, we're just like you: yopros looking for a good beer and a place to watch a great game, often times on a budget. Our goal is to review as many bars in the D.C. area as possible so that you can enjoy that big game.


Want more?

If the bar you're looking to check out is currently in our reviews, let us know! We'll check it out and give our personal opinions on the place.
